navigation commands for VIM :
	h is for <- this direction
	j is for down 
	k is for up 
	l is for right (->) 
	
	navigating entire line 
	cntr + e is for moving window UP with single line
	cntr + y is for moving window DOWN with single line
	$ is for moving to the END of the line  
	^ is for moving to the BEGINNING of the line 

	navigating with word
	b is for beginning of the PREVIOUS word
	e is for END of this word 
	w is for BEGINNIG of next word
